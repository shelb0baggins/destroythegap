WOMEN ARE THE MINORITY
IN COMPUTING       FUTURE

The statistics are astounding. Computing jobs are held by a large majority of males. Why? Well, many people argue that "girls just dont like to code" or "we aren't smart enough for that". That doesn't sound quite right. It's time to think differently.

Women are born and raised to do certain things. It's a little thing called "nature vs. nurture". Most young girls are told that they need to be a nurse or a teacher (which are most amazing, neccesary professions). However, what happens when we try to break the mold? If us girls start asking for video games for Christmas we hear "those are for boys". Why? Why does using hand-eye coordination and strategical skills have to be a boy thing?

The quick answer? It doesn't. Women are breaking boundaries everyday in fields like computer science, video-game design, and web development. A huge reason why girls don't go into tech is because they don't have role models. Those times are changing. Women are becoming CEOs of big tech companies, lead software engineers, and hopefully one day president. We can do anything!
